About trust & safety ratings

Every rental on RentSure is scored based on:

  • Trust score: Owner response time, complaints history, and rental agreement completion
  • Safety score: Locality ratings, CCTV coverage, police proximity, and public transport access
  • Budget fit: How well the rental matches your budget
  • Distance fit: Proximity to colleges and workplaces
